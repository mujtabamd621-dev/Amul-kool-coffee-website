Inquiries.

Clarity on our craft, ingredients, and the Velvet Roast experience.

Is Amul Kool Café best served chilled?

expand_more

Absolutely. To experience the full depth of the Velvet Roast and the silken texture of our premium milk, we recommend serving it ice cold. The condensation on the glass bottle is part of the sensory journey.

Do you use real dairy?

expand_more

Yes, Amul Kool Café is crafted exclusively with pure, high-quality Amul milk. It provides the essential creamy foundation that perfectly balances our robust espresso notes.

What is the shelf life of the glass bottles?

expand_more

Unopened, our bottles retain their peak flavor profile for up to 6 months when stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, please refrigerate and consume within 24 hours.
